Who we are

Bright Grammar International Schools is located in the heart of Mogadishu city-Somalia along Benadir street next to Hormuud University Hodan district.
The school offers Day care, Nursery, Primary and Secondary education.
Besides providing standard education guided by the curriculum, the school provides Quran studies to all learners so as to produce morally and spiritually upright students. Bright Grammar Internatinal Schools also imparts critical life skills among all learners. It empowers learners to develop their confidence, develop critical, analytical and optimistic character as well as empowering them to grow into good citizens.
